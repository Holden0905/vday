Roses are red,
Violets are blue,

Especially for you.

in this breath, you are present
and you
present the best
in me
like a mirror
at the surface of placid waters

i sink deep
down beneath the superficial
diving to the end
of what we can see
forever searching for...
some thing...
some one...
some time...

when things were more
or less
than what they are now.
some space...
where it's ok
to be me
to be here.

when you wrap me in the blanket of your compassion
i feel the harmony of god's whistle.
you bring me closer to heaven, to my humanity
with every moment we spend together.
